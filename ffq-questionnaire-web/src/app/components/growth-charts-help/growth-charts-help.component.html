Growth Charts' Help

Child Personal Information

The Child Personal Information panel is used to identify which child of the partent will be selected to display or modify the child's information. The Child Personal panel has to menus: Children and Gender. Both menus are required, meaning that for interacting with the other panels it is necessary to fill up the data in the Child Personal Information Panel first. The menu Children allows the parent to select the child first name, and the menu Gender identifies which charts must be displayed. The data for the charts are different depending on the gender selected.

The menu Children will display all the children of the parent.

The menu Gender will display the following options: male and female. The parent should select accordingly to their children's gender to guarantine reliable data and results of the charts interpretation.

Child Body Measurements

The Child Body Measurements panel is used to determine the type of units of measurements to use, add and save data along the time of the children as height and weight to plot them on the different type of charts. The data required consit of age, height and weight of the child. The valid values for age are on the interval from zero month to 24 months. The wight cannot be lesser than 1 kilogram or greater than 30 Kilograms. The height cannot be lesser than 40 centimeters and greater than 110 centimeters. The data of charts is retrieve from the World Health Organization and by default is expressed in Centimeters and Kilograms.
